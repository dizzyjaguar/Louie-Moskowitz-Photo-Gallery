Louie Moskowitz was born into a family of painters and ceramicists, his family moved to Humboldt County in a rural town called Redway. He stated taking pictures when he was 17 and is just getting around to understanding how to photograph his hometown. Louie Moskowitz is a 26 year old photographer based in Redway, in Humboldt County. He photographs the underdogs of the community rather than the rich pot growers and growers of the region. He chooses to take pictures of the buildings and local hangouts where the true underbelly of the community lies. He always was an avid corn eater, he always tried to eat it for almost every meal. At some point in his career someone suggested that he try a new food like oregano but that ended up not happening. Any way that was just the begining of his career. This drew a large amount of inspiration in his photography and thats why he mostly takes photos of vegetables.
